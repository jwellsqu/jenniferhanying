About Jen

A life spent helping people rise.

I'm Jen — a coach, education consultant, yoga teacher, humanitarian, and soon-to-be nurse. The through-line in everything I do is the same: I'm here to help people navigate their biggest transitions and become more fully themselves.

My path has been anything but linear. I've worked in refugee response, spent years on the front lines of women's rights work, taught yoga, traveled to 50+ countries, earned multiple degrees, and built a coaching practice that has transformed hundreds of lives. And now I'm heading to nursing school — because it turns out, when you're drawn to helping people heal and grow, there are infinite ways to answer that call.

I bring all of that to my work. When we work together — whether you're a student navigating college applications or a professional redesigning your life — you get someone who has lived transition, who has sat with uncertainty, and who knows that on the other side of a bold decision is a life that surprises you.

I believe deeply that life is meant to be lived passionately, and that clarity of vision is the first step to getting there. That's what I help you build.
